Interview Rock Garden
PROJECT 1
PROJECT 2
This project was designed to challenge students to work with typography on the internet and the basic HTML and CSS. We each worked with a partner and interviewed 10 unique questions. We created a website that houses the interview and introduces our partner to the class.
For this project, we created our own “rock garden” in the browser. We developed a typology of our own demonstrated with any media – photo, graphic, writing, videos, or a mix of different types. We included 20 “rocks” in it and housed on a single page or multi-page website.
